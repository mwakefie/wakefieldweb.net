DonutDunk Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

DonutDunk ("the App") is developed by Matthew Wakefield. This privacy policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to servers operated by the developer. The App does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information as described below.

Advertising (iOS only)

The App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, AdMob may collect the following types of information:

  • Device identifiers — such as the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), used for ad personalization and measurement
  • Coarse location — approximate location derived from IP address, used for ad targeting
  • Usage data — ad impressions, taps, and interactions
  • Diagnostics — crash data and performance metrics related to ad delivery

Before any tracking occurs, the App requests your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. If you decline, AdMob will serve non-personalized ads and will not use your device identifier for tracking across apps.

You can permanently remove all advertising by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase. Once purchased, the AdMob SDK will no longer load or display ads.

For more information on how Google handles your data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Center

The App integrates with Apple Game Center for leaderboards. Game Center is operated entirely by Apple. Your Game Center display name and scores are managed by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not have access to your Game Center account information.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a "Remove Ads" in-app purchase processed by Apple through the App Store. All payment information is handled by Apple. The developer does not receive or store any payment details.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad content is not specifically targeted at children.

Data Retention

The developer does not collect or retain any user data. For data collected by AdMob and Game Center, please refer to the respective privacy policies of Google and Apple linked above.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
